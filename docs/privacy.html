Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 3, 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Google Drive Video/Audio Downloader Chrome extension ("we", "our", or "the extension") handles information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Important: This extension does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, user information, or browsing history. All processing happens locally in your browser.

Data Collection

We do NOT collect any data.

  • No personal information is collected
  • No browsing history is stored
  • No usage statistics are tracked
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No analytics or tracking services are used

How the Extension Works

The extension operates entirely locally in your browser:

  • It intercepts network requests from Google Drive video player pages
  • It identifies the largest video and audio stream URLs
  • When you click the extension icon, it downloads these files directly to your computer
  • All processing happens in your browser - no data leaves your device

Permissions Explained

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • webRequest: Required to intercept Google Drive video stream requests to identify download URLs
  • tabs: Required to identify which browser tab contains the Google Drive video
  • downloads: Required to download the video and audio files to your computer
  • <all_urls>: Required to access Google Drive video URLs (only used when you're on a Google Drive page)

These permissions are used only for the extension's core functionality. They are not used to collect or transmit any data.

Data Storage

The extension uses Chrome's local storage API to temporarily store video and audio URLs only while you're using the extension. This data is:

  • Stored locally on your device only
  • Deleted when you close the browser tab
  • Never transmitted to any external server
  • Never shared with third parties

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, or external APIs. It only communicates with Google Drive to download video and audio files when you explicitly request a download by clicking the extension icon.

Children's Privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children. Since we don't collect data, this is not applicable.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
